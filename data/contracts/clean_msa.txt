MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Acme Corp and Beta LLC, effective as of January 1, 2026.

1. Limitation of Liability
Each party's aggregate liability arising out of or related to this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

2. Indemnification
Each party shall indemnify and hold the other harmless against third-party claims arising from its breach of confidentiality obligations or its infringement of intellectual property rights. These indemnification obligations are mutual and subject to the limitation of liability above.

3. Confidentiality
Each party shall protect the other party's Confidential Information using no less than reasonable care, and shall not disclose such proprietary information for a period of three (3) years following disclosure.

4. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon thirty (30) days' written notice if the breach remains uncured, and may terminate for convenience upon sixty (60) days' written notice.

5. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.

6. Payment
Customer shall pay all undisputed fees within net thirty (30) days of the invoice date.
