MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into by and between Orion Labs and Vega Systems, effective as of March 1, 2026.

1. Confidentiality
Each party shall protect the other party's Confidential Information and proprietary information using reasonable care and shall not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent.

2. Limitation of Liability
Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed two times (2x) the total annual fees payable under the related order form.

3. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon thirty (30) days' written notice, and for convenience upon sixty (60) days' written notice.

4. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware.
