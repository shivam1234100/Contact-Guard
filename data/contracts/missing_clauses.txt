CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of April 1, 2026 (the "Effective Date") by and between Gamma Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Consultant"), and Delta Co., a Texas corporation ("Client").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Consultant provides management consulting and advisory services; and

WHEREAS, Client wishes to retain Consultant to perform the services described herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

1. Engagement and Services
Client retains Consultant to perform the management consulting services described in Exhibit A (the "Services"). Consultant shall perform the Services on a best-efforts basis at times mutually agreed by the Parties and shall determine the manner and means by which the Services are performed.

2. Fees and Payment
In consideration of the Services, Client shall pay Consultant the fees set forth in Exhibit A. Consultant shall invoice Client monthly, and Client shall pay all invoiced amounts within net thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

3. Expenses
Client shall reimburse Consultant for reasonable, pre-approved travel and out-of-pocket expenses incurred in performing the Services, provided that Consultant submits appropriate receipts.

4. Term
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for a period of one (1) year, after which it may be renewed only by the mutual written agreement of the Parties.

5. Independent Contractor
Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, or agent of Client. Consultant is solely responsible for its own taxes, insurance, and benefits, and has no authority to bind Client.
